How it works
01

Text Vectorisation

Your review is converted into a TF-IDF vector — a mathematical representation that weighs important words and downplays common filler words.

02

Logistic Regression

A trained binary classifier analyses the vector, comparing it against patterns learned from thousands of labelled genuine and fake reviews.

03

Confidence Score

The model outputs a probability score (0–100%) representing its certainty. Higher is more reliable. You also see which words triggered the result.

TruthLens uses NLP + Logistic Regression. Always use your own judgment when making purchases.
